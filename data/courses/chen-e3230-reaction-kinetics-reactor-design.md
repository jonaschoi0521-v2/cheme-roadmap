## Why This Course Matters

How fast do reactions happen, and how do you design the vessel to run them? This is the engineering heart of pharmaceutical synthesis — batch reactors, continuous stirred tanks, plug flow reactors. Understanding selectivity and yield is critical for drug manufacturing where impurities matter enormously.
