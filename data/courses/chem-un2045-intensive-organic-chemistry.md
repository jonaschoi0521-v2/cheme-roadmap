## Why This Course Matters

Organic chemistry is the language of drug molecules. Reaction mechanisms, synthesis pathways, functional group transformations — this is the foundation for understanding how drugs are made and how they interact with biological systems. Taking the intensive track accelerates access to advanced pharma-relevant chemistry.
