## Why This Course Matters

Heat transfer, mass transfer, and fluid flow are the three pillars of how anything moves through a process. In bioprocessing and pharmaceutical manufacturing, transport phenomena govern drug dissolution rates, bioreactor mixing, membrane filtration, and drug delivery in the body. This is one of the most directly relevant courses to the pharma/biotech mission.
