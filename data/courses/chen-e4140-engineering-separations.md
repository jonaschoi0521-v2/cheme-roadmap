## Why This Course Matters

Purification is one of the most expensive steps in drug manufacturing. Chromatography, distillation, extraction, membrane filtration — these are how you get a pure drug out of a messy reaction mixture. Understanding separations deeply is essential for designing cost-effective pharmaceutical processes, particularly for underserved markets where cost is everything.
