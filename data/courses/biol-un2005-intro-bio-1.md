## Why This Course Matters

Biochemistry and molecular biology are the science underneath biotechnology and biologics manufacturing. Understanding gene expression, protein synthesis, and enzyme kinetics is essential for working on biopharmaceuticals — which is where the biggest unmet medical needs are for underserved populations.
