## Why This Course Matters

Replaces the PHYS GU4021/4022 Quantum Mechanics sequence. The Orgo co-registration
policy in Sem 1 pushed intro physics into sophomore year, which delayed PHYS UN3003
(Mechanics) and UN3007 (E&M) — both hard prerequisites for PHYS Quantum Mechanics.
Quantum Chemistry does **not** require 3003/3007, so it decouples the quantum sequence
from the delayed physics chain.

It is also better targeted: Quantum Chemistry (Hartree–Fock, basis sets, electronic
structure) is the direct foundation of the molecular-modeling / electronic-structure
world (Friesner, Schrödinger) this roadmap aims at — more so than abstract QM formalism.
