## Why This Course Matters

Thermodynamics determines what reactions are possible, what equilibria look like, and how much energy a process requires. In pharmaceutical manufacturing, thermodynamics governs crystallization, solubility (critical for drug formulation), and distillation. Every process design decision is grounded here.
